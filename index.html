About

The CPS Research Seminar I is a weekly gathering where the participants of the course share their ongoing work, explore new ideas, and engage in open discussion. The seminar is intentionally broad and anything at the intersection of AI, machine learning, and robotics is welcome.

Presentations can take many forms: paper walkthroughs, thesis progress updates, early-stage research ideas, live demos, or simply a talk on a topic you found interesting. There is no strict format and the participants can bring whatever they want to present and let the discussion flow from there.
